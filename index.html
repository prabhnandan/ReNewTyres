Who We Are

Dedicated To Sustainability & Performance

Founded in 2010, ReNew Tyres has been at the forefront of the tyre restoration industry. We combine advanced technology with traditional craftsmanship to deliver superior results.

Our team of certified technicians has over 30 years of combined experience in tyre restoration, ensuring that every tyre we restore meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

We're committed to reducing waste and promoting sustainability in the automotive industry. By restoring tyres instead of replacing them, we help reduce the environmental impact of tyre production and disposal.
